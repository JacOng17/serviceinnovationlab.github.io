The Service Innovation Lab

The Lab opened 2017 and closed mid-2020. The resources inside have been archived in a way that ensures they can provide ongoing value to those who follow. Some Lab projects had an international impact; some were small, yet incremental in contributing to bigger changes that benefited New Zealanders. As such, this Toolkit represents a repository of immense value. You are free to copy.
